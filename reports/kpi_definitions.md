## Total Revenue
Sum of all successful payment values.
Used to measure overall business performance.

## Monthly Revenue Growth
Month-over-month percentage change in revenue.
Used to track growth trends and seasonality.

## Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Total revenue generated by a customer over their lifetime.
Used to identify high-value customers.

## Repeat Purchase Rate
Percentage of customers who placed more than one order.
Used to measure customer loyalty.

## Churn Rate
Percentage of customers who did not return after first purchase.
Used to understand retention challenges.
